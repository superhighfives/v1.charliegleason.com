  • Anatole

  • A WebGL-powered home for the Sydney based orchestral laptop maestro.

A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

I a big fan of Tommy Faith, having followed his blog Sound Doctrine for a really long time.

When he got in touch and asked if I would been keen to help Anatole, from his Teef Records label, to bring his EP to life I jumped at the opportuntity. I realised we could use WebGL via Three.js to make a dynamic reprentation of the artwork that stayed true to the original direction.

It was a bunch of fun.
