  • Unbound

  • A redesign for the crowdfunding book publisher, Unbound.

A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

Unbound is a Rails-powered crowdfunding platform connecting authors and readers. Authors present a pitch, readers pledge, and when the goal is reached the book is written. Over ten weeks I worked with the team at Unbound to relaunch the site, looking after design, front-end development and integration.

You should absolutely find a book to pledge, and get involved.
